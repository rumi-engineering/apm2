# Theory-of-Mind / Mental-State Reasoning

**Category:** Strategic and Social Reasoning

## What it is

Infer beliefs, intentions, knowledge states of others (nested beliefs).

## What it outputs

- Behavior predictions
- Communication strategies
- Coordination plans
- Understanding of others' perspectives

## How it differs

Focuses on beliefs-about-beliefs; often essential for collaboration. Not just what others will do, but what they think you think they'll do.

## Best for

- Leadership
- UX
- Teamwork
- Threat modeling
- Communication design

## Common failure mode

Mind-reading with overconfidence; projecting your incentives onto others. You're often wrong about what others are thinking.
